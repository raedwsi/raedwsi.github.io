Level 1

Curriculum aligned to the pre A1 CEFR and Global Scale of English standards. Students will learn the alphabet, the spelling of simple words, basic English vocabulary and conversation.

More

Level 2

Curriculum aligned to A1 CEFR and Global Scale of English standards. Students will learn to interact in meaningful ways, to write simple sentences and read short descriptions.

More

Level 3

Curriculum aligned to A2 CEFR and Global Scale of English standards. Students will learn to describe people, places and events, ask and answer questions about familiar topics and read and write paragraphs.

More

Level 4

Curriculum aligned to B1 CERF and Global Scale of English standard. Students will learn to describe and give reasons and explanations for opinions, talk about prepared topics and read and write connected texts.
